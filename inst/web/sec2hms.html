Convert Seconds to h:m:s

Usage

sec2hms(x)

Arguments

x
A vector of times in seconds.

Convert Seconds to h:m:s

Value

Returns a vector of times in h:m:s format.

Description

Converts a vector of seconds to h:m:s.

Examples

sec2hms(c(256, 3456, 56565))



[1] 00:04:16 00:57:36 15:42:45
